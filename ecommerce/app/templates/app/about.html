About Us


FunkimodMarket -online is an online sales system for various products, You can sell or buy online without traveling and you receive the product sold or purchased by courier. To facilitate the arrival or shipment of products, we are currently working in the city of Nairobi in Kenya and in the city of Gitega in Burundi. In other cities of Burundi or Kenya the products can also be exchanged but with a small delay. Please contact us at our addresses listed above!
